Tibinator Application (hereinafter referred to as "App"), is an application developed to assist in managing the tuberculosis outbreaks in Malaysia. It allows you (hereinafter referred to as "User") to perform a self-health assessment, monitor their health status and share it with the local health authority to enable immediate actions in providing treatments if required.

Personal Information

The app will not record user's Personal Data, as the personal data is already consented when the user register with the administrator/care provider. Only symptoms information will be collected, and will be used for self-care and monitoring by the respecitive health care provider. This information is not shared with other organizations for other purposes unless specifically stated.

Data Collected

This app collects data with the consent of the user and is done voluntarily through, but not limited to, answering health assessments, and contacting app support via email or technical support form.

Data Confidentiality

Data collected by the App will be kept confidential in accordance with this Privacy Policy in accordance with any applicable laws which may take effect from time to time.
